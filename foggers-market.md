<p><strong>The Foggers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Foggers</li><li>Cold Foggers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The foggers market is categorized mainly into thermal foggers and cold foggers. Thermal foggers generate fog using heat to vaporize insecticides, resulting in dense, long-lasting fog clouds, ideal for outdoor pest control. Cold foggers, on the other hand, use airflow mechanisms to disperse liquids into fine droplets without heating, making them suitable for indoor use and lower concentrations of chemicals. Both types serve distinct applications across agriculture, urban pest control, and public health, catering to varied user needs.</p></p>

<p><p>Single-Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT) is a nuclear imaging technique that provides 3D images of tissue function and metabolism. The market comprises two main types: Single Imaging Gamma Cameras, which capture 2D images and require advanced processing to create 3D representations, and SPECT/CT systems, which combine SPECT imaging with computed tomography for better anatomical localization and enhanced diagnostic accuracy. This integration allows for detailed functional and structural mapping, improving patient care and treatment planning.</p></p>

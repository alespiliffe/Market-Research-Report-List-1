<p><p>The screw conveyor market can be categorized based on installation orientation: horizontal, inclined, and vertical. Horizontal installations are commonly used for transporting bulk materials over long distances with low elevation changes. Inclined installations facilitate the movement of materials at various angles, making them suitable for conveying materials to different heights. Vertical installations are designed for high-efficiency transport in confined spaces, often used in applications requiring significant elevation gain. Each type serves distinct operational needs and industry requirements.</p></p>

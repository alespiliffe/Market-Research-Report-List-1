<p><p>Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) Equipment encompasses various techniques used to evaluate materials without causing damage. Eddy Current Testing employs electromagnetic induction to detect flaws in conductive materials. Magnetic Flux Leakage Testing identifies defects in ferromagnetic products by measuring magnetic field variations. Ultrasonic Testing uses high-frequency sound waves to reveal internal discontinuities in materials. Other market types may include radiographic testing, acoustic emission testing, and visual inspection, each offering unique advantages for assessing material integrity across various industries.</p></p>
